NESTLE PACKAGING REDESIGN

Team: Yiokling Chu, Thai Van Nguyen.

Here we consider how to assess the quality of the nestle package design, we made informed design decisions to redesign it.

For this, we redesigned the product package (the box itself) as well as the logo of the company.

    For the redesign we considered:

  • Assessing good and bad design elements.
  • The client (company that makes the product) and the consumer (person or community who buys the product).
  • The Color schemes.
  • Composition.
  • Typography.
  • Logos and branding.
  • Creating a character
