SOLITARY ROOM COLLECTION

This collection was inspired by the confinement and solitude given during pandemic times. I wanted to convey the idea of feeling loneliness and finding life on particular items in your surroundings.

A crucial part of the Solitary Room Collection was choosing which objects represented optimism in an isolated room. I chose a book, a source of peace, being immersed in another world. A lamp that radiates a warm and calming light on empty rooms. A doorknob symbolizing the joy of going out after plenty of days inside.

I decided to use blue and yellow, colors with high contrast on hue to create an impact.
