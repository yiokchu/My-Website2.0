TEA PACKAGING

Next, is the package design for a collection of flower teas (Jasmine Tea, Rose Tea, Lavender Tea, Hibiscus Tea, Chamomile Tea). This product collection suggests a clear, clean, and calming personality...

For this project, I decided to use pastel colors to give the packages a feel of lightness, softness, and calmness. This matches the product, it gives it a feeling of earthiness and sophistication.

I also decided to use flat icons to focus on color and shape. Additionally, making the illustrations flow between packages was a decision made to give consistency, allowing the packages all to look like a unified collection.

I designed everything from the outside packages to the individual tea packages.
