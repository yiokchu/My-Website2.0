EYE CATCHING COLLECTION

It is often said that eyes are a window to the soul, they are a portal establishing a connection with others.

I wanted to capture a glance of life, reflecting emotions in indeterminate moments.
I love the idea of grasping a gaze, an occasion. Eyes... something so beautiful and unique to each person, something so important but so overlooked.
